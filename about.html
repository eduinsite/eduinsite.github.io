About Us

Dr. Edward Emling has been in the psychoeducational assessment business in the Bay Area since 1979. His extensive career includes positions as a teacher, counselor, school psychologist, Director of special education, and graduate level professor. Dr. Emling is a specialist in the area of learning style evaluation, and frequently lends his vast knowledge and expertise to parent groups, pediatricians, and child-centered organizations.
Dr. Brittany Stevens has been a staff psychologist for Educational Insight since 1999. Dr. Stevens has amassed many years of experience as a special education teacher, school psychologist, and graduate level professor. She possesses an impressive research background in developmental and educational psychology and often consults with parent groups, private schools, and school districts on a variety of psychoeducational issues.
